Who We Are

A group for like-minded people to build strength and power through community during troubled times. We welcome progressives and leftists of every type! Come get to know other progressives! Have fun and relax with other politically and socially progressive people in a variety of different environments! We'll be having pub nights, movie nights, game nights, pizza nights, dinner parties, and anything else that sounds like a fun night. Brainstorm, network, or just enjoy the activities with other people you'll probably like!

Mission Statement

1. Building solidarity and community strength means having each other’s back. It’s my personal hope for this group that if fascism takes something from one of us, our members respond in kind with mutual-aid, useful resources, community support, and companionship. One for all, and all for one.

2. Helping to create community leaders, activists, and influencers that help shape society and move it to the left. We want to empower our members to be the change.
